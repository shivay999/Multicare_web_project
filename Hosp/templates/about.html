ABOUT

The main motive of this website is to provide a reliable service to our users with a guaranteed privacy. This website holds the details of the patient to book their appointment with the doctors and can view their previous appointments, prescriptions, reports etc.

The patient can view the doctors of their specialization, so that they can book an appointment with the respective doctor of their interest. The doctor can view all the details of the appointments so that he can give the prescription for the patients. The prescription details are also saved so that whenever the patient again visits to show the previous treatment taken.

The Receptionist in the organization will handle the outpatients coming to the hospital and maintain the payments for the consultation through offline. The Admin can add new Doctors, Receptionists, view all the appointments, Delete Doctor account, view the prescriptions given to the patients by doctors.
